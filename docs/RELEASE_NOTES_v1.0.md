# FrontPorchAI v1.0 Release Notes

## Title

**AI on the Front Porch: A Family Bridge for Understanding Artificial Intelligence**

## Version

v1.0

## DOI

https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20466505

## Release summary

FrontPorchAI v1.0 is a plainspoken family education resource for helping skeptical, rural, faith-shaped, practical, or family-centered readers understand artificial intelligence without fear, hype, or blind trust.

The project frames AI as a careful working partner: not a human being, not a soul, not a moral authority, and not a replacement for family, faith, conscience, privacy, or responsibility.

## What is included

- One-page family handout

- Front porch analogies
- Safe first demonstrations

- Share kit

- Reflection questions
- Closing manifesto

## Core sentence

> AI is not replacing my heart; it is helping me use my heart better — when I keep truth, love, privacy, humility, and human responsibility in charge.

## Core boundary

> AI can help, but it does not rule.

## Resource type

Educational resource / position paper. This is not a scientific study, medical guidance, legal guidance, financial advice, or a replacement for human judgment.

## Authorship transparency

Human-directed and human-owned by Michael W. Hughes. AI assistance was used for drafting, organizing, refinement, and review. Final responsibility remains human.
